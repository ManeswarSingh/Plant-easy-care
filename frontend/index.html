About Us

Welcome to Plant Easy, an innovative project aimed at transforming how we detect and prevent plant diseases. Our goal is to develop an efficient and automated plant disease detection system using advanced Machine Learning techniques. By leveraging TensorFlow and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), we can accurately identify a wide range of plant diseases from leaf images. Our model is trained on a robust dataset sourced from Kaggle, which includes numerous plant species and their associated diseases. Plant Easy demonstrates the power of machine learning in modernizing plantation practices, making disease detection tools more accessible and effective. Through our system, we are committed to improving plant health, enhancing food security, and promoting sustainable agricultural practices for a better future.
